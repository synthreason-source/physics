
# how much destructive power does it have

If you manage to bypass the safety features of a 1,000-watt microwave oven and use it to ignite a magnetically confined Electron Cyclotron Resonance (ECR) plasma inside a millimeter-scale woodpile crystal, the resulting destructive power is extreme. However, it is not "destructive" in the sense of a bomb or an explosion; rather, it acts simultaneously as a **hyper-focused thermal blowtorch** and an **unshielded radiation cannon**.

Here is exactly how much destructive power this system possesses:

## 1. Thermal Destructive Power (The Plasma Blowtorch)

The primary physical destruction comes from the **power density** of the plasma.

* A standard microwave spreads its 1,000 watts of power across the entire internal volume of the oven (roughly 30,000 cubic centimeters) to gently heat food.
* By using a woodpile crystal, you are funneling that entire 1,000 watts into a void that is only a few cubic millimeters in size.
* **The Result:** The absorbed power density inside the crystal reaches between **400 and 1,000 watts per cubic centimeter**. This is an astronomical concentration of heat, equivalent to the focal point of an industrial steel-cutting laser. The intense thermal shock and plasma sputtering will instantly flash-melt, vaporize, or shatter almost any known material—including advanced ceramics or synthetic diamond—placed inside the void.[^1]


## 2. Radiological Destructive Power (The X-Ray Cannon)

The invisible and far more dangerous destructive power is the radiation. When the 30,000-volt (30 keV) electrons slam into the walls of the crystal, they rapidly decelerate and emit Bremsstrahlung X-rays.[^2]

* Even if the X-ray conversion efficiency is incredibly low (e.g., only 0.1% to 1%), a 1,000-watt plasma will generate between **1 to 10 solid watts of continuous X-ray emission**.[^3]
* **The Dose Rate:** At point-blank range (within a few feet of the microwave door, which cannot block these X-rays), the radiation dose rate from an unshielded 1-kW ECR source can easily exceed **1 to 10 Gray per hour (Gy/h)** or Sieverts per hour (Sv/h).[^4][^5][^3]
* **The Biological Impact:** To put this in perspective, the maximum safe *annual* dose for a radiation worker is 0.05 Sieverts. Standing in front of this microwave for just **3 to 5 minutes** would deliver a lethal dose of radiation, leading to severe acute radiation sickness, cellular necrosis (tissue melting), and highly probable death.


## 3. Electromagnetic Destructive Power (The EMP Effect)

As the crystal vaporizes and the plasma density spikes past the microwave's critical density limit, the plasma ball will act like a solid mirror.

* **Reflected Power:** Nearly 100% of the 1,000-watt microwave beam will bounce off the plasma and reflect straight back into the magnetron antenna.
* **The Result:** This sudden influx of reverse energy will cause massive voltage standing waves inside the magnetron's waveguide. The 4,000-volt internal circuitry of the microwave will experience catastrophic electrical arcing, physically melting the magnetron's copper antenna and destroying the appliance in a localized, fiery electrical short.

In short, this device will not blow up your house, but it will instantly vaporize its target, permanently destroy the microwave oven, and kill anyone standing in the room with lethal doses of X-ray radiation.
