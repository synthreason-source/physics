The three rotameric wells are not equal depth — the chiral centre at Cα breaks the symmetry. Clockwise vs counterclockwise rotation from any given starting point costs different amounts of energy. This means:

Positive rotation of the indole (clockwise when viewed along Cβ-Cγ) costs X energy
Negative rotation (counterclockwise) costs Y energy
X ≠ Y because the molecule is chiral

When the dihedral rotates, this electric dipole physically moves through space.
