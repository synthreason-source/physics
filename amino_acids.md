## Amino Acids With Poor/No BBB Crossing & Their CNS-Relevant Effects

| Amino Acid | BBB Crossing | Why Excluded | CNS Effect if Delivered |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Glycine** | Poor | No LAT1 affinity, GlyT transporters minimal at BBB | Inhibitory neurotransmitter, NMDA co-agonist, reduces excitotoxicity |
| **GABA** | Very poor — actively excluded | No specific BBB transporter, efflux pumps | Inhibitory neurotransmitter, anxiolytic, anti-excitotoxic |
| **Taurine** | Poor | TauT transporter poorly expressed at BBB | Neuroprotective, osmoregulatory, anti-excitotoxic, membrane stabilizing |
| **Glutamate** | Actively excluded | Efflux transporters protect CNS from excess | Excitatory neurotransmitter — excess causes excitotoxicity |
| **Aspartate** | Actively excluded | Same as glutamate | Excitatory — neurotoxic in excess |
| **Lysine** | Poor | CAT transporters poorly expressed at BBB | Carnitine synthesis, collagen repair, antiviral |
| **Arginine** | Limited | CAT-1 transporter — restricted | Nitric oxide synthesis, neuroprotection, vasodilation |
| **Proline** | Poor | No specific BBB transporter | Collagen synthesis, glutamate precursor, osmoprotection |
| **Hydroxyproline** | Very poor | No transporter | Collagen stabilization, limited CNS role known |
| **Ornithine** | Poor | No specific transporter | Urea cycle, polyamine synthesis — neuroprotective polyamines |
| **Citrulline** | Poor | No specific transporter | NO pathway, reduces neuroinflammation |
| **Beta-alanine** | Poor | Shares TauT — poorly expressed | Carnosine synthesis, buffering, antioxidant |
| **Carnosine** | Very poor — too large | Size exclusion | Antioxidant, anti-glycation, neuroprotective |
| **Theanine** | Limited | Partial LAT1 affinity only | Relaxation, GABA modulation, reduces glutamate excitotoxicity |
| **D-serine** | Limited | Separate transporter from L-serine | NMDA co-agonist — more potent than glycine at NR1 subunit |
| **Homocysteine** | Poor | No specific transporter | Neurotoxic in excess — excitotoxicity, oxidative stress |
| **Cysteine** | Limited | Partial transport — oxidized rapidly | Glutathione precursor, antioxidant — neuroprotective if delivered |
| **Carnitine** | Very poor | OCTN transporters minimal at BBB | Mitochondrial fatty acid transport, neuroprotection, energy metabolism |
| **Acetylcarnitine** | Moderate — better than carnitine | Slightly more lipophilic | Acetylcholine precursor, mitochondrial support, neuroprotective |
